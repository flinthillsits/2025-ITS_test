EV Service Patrol Vehicle Operations Functional Object

Description

'EV Service Patrol Vehicle Operations' provides on–board processing and communications to service patrol vehicles that reduce response times and improve safety of responding personnel. It supports service patrol vehicle dispatch and provides incident information back to the dispatching center.

Included In

Riley County Emergency Vehicles
KHP Troop C Vehicles
Junction City Emergency Vehicles
Geary County Emergency Vehicles
Pottawatomie County Emergency Vehicles
Manhattan Emergency Vehicles
Blue Township Fire Department Vehicles
