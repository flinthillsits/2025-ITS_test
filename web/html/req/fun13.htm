TMC Traffic Information Dissemination Functional Object

Description

'TMC Traffic Information Dissemination' disseminates traffic and road conditions, closure and detour information, incident information, driver advisories, and other traffic–related data to other centers, the media, and driver information systems. It monitors and controls driver information system field equipment including dynamic message signs and highway advisory radio, managing dissemination of driver information through these systems.

Included In

Manhattan Traffic Operations Facility
Junction City Engineering Department
KDOT District 1 Office
KDOT District 2 Office
KDOT Statewide TOC
