Transit Vehicle On-Board Trip Monitoring Functional Object

Description

'Transit Vehicle On–Board Trip Monitoring' tracks vehicle location, monitors fuel usage, collects operational status (doors opened/closed, running times, etc.) and sends the collected, time stamped data to the Transit Management Center.

Included In

FHATA On–Board Security Cameras

Functional Requirements

IDRequirementUser Defined
1The transit vehicle shall track the current location of the transit vehicle.False
2The transit vehicle shall support the computation of the location of a transit vehicle using on–board sensors to augment the location determination function. This may include proximity to the transit stops or other known reference points as well as recording trip length.False
3The transit vehicle shall record transit trip monitoring data including vehicle mileage and fuel usage.False
4The transit vehicle shall record transit trip monitoring data including operational status information such as doors open/closed, running times, etc.False
5The transit vehicle shall send the transit vehicle trip monitoring data to center–based trip monitoring functions.False
6The transit vehicle shall receive transit stop requests from travelers.False
7The transit vehicle shall receive transit stop requests from Transit OperationsFalse
