MCM Vehicle Tracking Functional Object

Description

'MCM Vehicle Tracking' tracks the location of maintenance and construction vehicles and other equipment. Vehicle/equipment location and associated information is presented to the operator.

Included In

Manhattan Street Division Office
Junction City Public Works Department
Pottawatomie County Public Works

Functional Requirements

IDRequirementUser Defined
