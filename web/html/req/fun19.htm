Roadway Basic Surveillance Functional Object

Description

'Roadway Basic Surveillance' monitors traffic conditions using fixed equipment such as loop detectors and CCTV cameras.

Included In

Manhattan CCTV Cameras
Junction City CCTV Cameras
Manhattan Traffic Detection Equipment
KDOT CCTV Cameras
Junction City Traffic Detection Equipment

Functional Requirements

IDRequirementUser Defined
1The field element shall collect, process, digitize, and send traffic sensor data (speed, volume, and occupancy) to the center for further analysis and storage, under center control.False
2The field element shall collect, process, and send traffic images to the center for further analysis and distribution.False
3The field element shall collect, digitize, and send multimodal crossing and high occupancy vehicle (HOV), and high occupancy toll (HOT) lane sensor data to the center for further analysis and storage.False
4The field element shall return sensor and CCTV system operational status to the controlling center.False
5The field element shall return sensor and CCTV system fault data to the controlling center for repair.False
6The field element shall collect, process, and send traffic images to the center for further wrong way vehicle entries analysis and detection.False
7The field element shall detect vehicles and vulnerable road users and share this information with roadside communications equipment. This supports detection of vehicles and vulnerable road users that do not have operable short range communications capabilities.False
