Description

Pottawatomie County Public Works Department is comprised of five divisions: Road and Bridge, Environmental Health, Solid Waste, Utilities, and Facilities. The Road & Bridge Division is responsible for maintenance of roads, bridges and drainage structures, and snow removal.
