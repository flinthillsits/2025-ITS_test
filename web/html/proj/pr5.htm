Road Weather Information System Stations

Description

This project will install additional road weather information system (RWIS) stations in the City of Manhattan. The City of Manhattan currently has two RWIS stations located on K–18 & 10th St and Kimball Ave & Candlewood. One additional RWIS, as well as the wireless or fiber links for communications, is planned on the east side of the city.

Status: In Progress

Timeframe: Short Term
