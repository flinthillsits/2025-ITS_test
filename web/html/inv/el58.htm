Pottawatomie County Maintenance and Construction Vehicles

Status: Existing

Description

This element represents Pottawatomie County's vehicles and equipment for roadway maintenance and construction purposes, including heavy equipment, trucks, supervisory vehicles, and snow plows. Most vehicles have radio communications and 55 vehicles utilize InTouch Automated Vehicle Location (AVL) equipment to allow for central monitoring of vehicle locations.
